Poise® Pads & Liners

Stay fresh and confident with Poise® pads and liners. They're designed specifically for Light Bladder Leakage (LBL),
making them an easy choice for the 1 in 3 women who experience LBL.

The protection you trust, in a shape you’ll love.

Designed with your curves in mind, new Poise® Pads with ContourFit™ shape give you coverage where it’s needed with less bunching in the middle. Plus, they provide up to 12 hours of protection, and stay 5x drier than the leading Always period pad†. What’s not to love?
